# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2, 2025**

ProcessDrive ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Live Attendance Tracking mobile application ("App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

When you use the App, we collect the following personal information:

| Data Type | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Employee ID | Your unique employee identifier |
| Name | Your full name as registered by your employer |
| PIN | Your authentication credential (stored securely) |
| Department | Your department within the organization |
| Job Position | Your job title or position |

### 1.2 Location Information

The App collects precise location data, including:

- **GPS Coordinates**: Latitude and longitude of your device
- **Timestamp**: Date and time of each location reading
- **Location Accuracy**: The precision level of the GPS reading

**When Location is Collected:**

- At the time of clock-in
- Every 60 seconds while you are clocked in
- At the time of clock-out
- Location tracking stops completely when you clock out

### 1.3 Device Information

We may collect information about your device, including:

- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Network information

### 1.4 Usage Information

We collect information about how you use the App:

- Clock-in and clock-out times
- Session duration
- App interaction patterns

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Primary Purposes

- **Authentication**: To verify your identity and grant access to the App
- **Attendance Tracking**: To record your clock-in and clock-out times
- **Location Verification**: To verify your presence at designated work locations
- **Workforce Management**: To help your employer manage field operations

### 2.2 Secondary Purposes

- **Reporting**: To generate attendance and location reports for management
- **Compliance**: To maintain records as required by labor laws
- **Safety**: To ensure employee safety during work hours
- **Service Improvement**: To improve App functionality and user experience

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 3.1 With Your Employer

All data collected through the App is shared with your employer, including:

- Attendance records
- Location data and history
- Clock-in/clock-out times

Your employer is the data controller and has access to all your App data through their Odoo system.

### 3.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share data with:

- **Odoo**: Your employer's ERP system where attendance data is stored
- **Cloud Providers**: For data storage and processing (as used by your employer)

### 3.3 We Do NOT Share Data With

- Advertisers or marketing companies
- Data brokers
- Social media platforms
- Any unrelated third parties

### 3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

- Court orders or legal processes
- Government requests
- Protection of our legal rights
- Emergency situations involving safety

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where Data is Stored

- **Local Device**: Minimal data stored locally (session info, preferences)
- **Company Server**: All attendance and location data is transmitted to and stored on your employer's Odoo server
- **Data Location**: Determined by your employer's server infrastructure

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures including:

- Secure HTTPS connections for data transmission
- Session-based authentication
- Encrypted storage of sensitive credentials
- Access controls and authentication

### 4.3 Data Retention

- Data retention periods are determined by your employer
- Upon employment termination, contact your employer regarding data deletion
- Local app data is deleted when you uninstall the App

## 6. Background Location Usage

### 6.1 Why We Use Background Location

The App uses background location to:

- Continue tracking your location while the App is minimized
- Ensure accurate attendance records during work hours
- Maintain continuous location updates for field employees

### 6.2 When Background Location is Active

- **Only when clocked in**: Background tracking starts at clock-in
- **Stops at clock-out**: Background tracking stops when you clock out
- **User controlled**: You control when tracking starts and stops

### 6.3 Battery Considerations

- The App is optimized to minimize battery usage
- Location updates are batched when possible
- You can monitor battery usage in your device settings
